Frugally.io

Welcome to the Frugally.io beta!

Frugally is a smarter online shopping experience. Minimize your time spent staring at a screen by having all of the best deals from all of your favorite vendors in one convenient place. Whats more, we have our own algorithms set in place to recommend deals based upon your previous shopping habits.

Frugally is a simple design that employs the use of Selenium's web scraping libraries to gather all of the best deals from a set list of vendors. By handing this data off to our LAMP server (Hardware owned!) and using it to power our AI algorithms, we are able to create a truly unique shopping experience that is curated to the user. All of Frugally.io's data is stored at home, right here in Philadelphia Pennsylvania.

You have been selected to participate in the Frugally.io beta, congrats! Not only will you have early access to our catalog, but you will be instrumental in our conversion to a stable 1.0 build. At the bottom of each page, you will find a suggestions box that sends us feedback based upon your user experience; we will use this feedback to compile a more personalized user experience in the march towards 1.0.

Cheers!
